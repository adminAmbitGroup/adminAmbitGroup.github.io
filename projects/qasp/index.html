Ambit BPA - Customer Feedback Application

A contractor metric is a performance or behavior measurement of an objective. Contractor metrics may identify performance levels and compare them against a target metric. When using contractor metrics as a method of surveillance, Team Ambit is responsible for reporting on its own performance based on the measurements provided by the government with contractor input.

Quality of Product or Service

Performance of standard Sprint quality if based on achieving identified backlog items and number of fixes needed on the sprint product.

  1. Unsatisfactory - Some backlog items not achieved; Major fixes.
  2. Marginal - Few backlog items not achieved; Some major fixes.
  3. Satisfactory - Backlog items achieved; Some minor fixes.
  4. Good - Backlog items achieved; Few minor fixes.
  5. Excellent - Product exceeds backlog items achieved; Few minor fixes.

Problem Resolution

Performance of standard The contractor is proactive and resolves problems in a timely and sound manner.

  1. Unsatisfactory - Inadequately resolves problems.
  2. Marginal - Significant agency help to resolve problems jeopardizing contract goals.
  3. Satisfactory - Good communications about problems and with agency help resolves contract goals.
  4. Good - Successfully overcome or resolve problems with minimal agency intervention.
  5. Excellent - Anticipates and avoids most problems and overcomes unforeseen problems.

Cost

Performance of standard Provide sound ROI and easy administration of costs.

  1. Unsatisfactory - Cost performance jeopardizes contract goals.
  2. Marginal - Returned below TO performance; billing issues.
  3. Satisfactory - Performed within costs; billing mostly on time and accurate.
  4. Good - Performed well within costs; billing on time and accurate.
  5. Exceeds - Cost return higher ROI than states in the TO; billing accurate and timely.

Timeliness of Performance

Performance of standard Timeliness of delivery of products and deliverables in aggregate.

  1. Unsatisfactory - Product delivery is at high tempo with a variety of small to complex products in development; Acceptance is high.
  2. Marginal - Product delivery is behind on TO deliverables with product acceptance.
  3. Satisfactory - Product delivery meets TO deliverables with product acceptance; Minor delays.
  4. Good - Product delivery is at a steady tempo; Product acceptance is good; On time deliveries.
  5. Excellent - Product delivery is at high tempo with a variety of small to complex products in development; Acceptance is high.

Business Relations

Performance of standard Contractor management performs the contract work effectively and efficiently.

  1. Unsatisfactory - Unethical or illegal business practices.
  2. Marginal - The contractor does not perform or interact with the client in a professional manner.
  3. Satisfactory - The contractor management performs the contract work and interacts with the client.
  4. Good - The contractor management performs contract work and interacts with the client in a professional manner.
  5. Excellent - Highly effective, proactive business practices.

Customer Service

Performance of standard Customer is satisfied by the overall service provided by the contractor.

  1. Unsatisfactory - Routinely late, ineffective, or rude. Customer expresses frustration or anger about interactions. Complains are unresolved. Contractor seems unaware of service issues.
  2. Marginal - Response is often late, ineffective, or rude. Some complaints are resolved.
  3. Satisfactory - Response is uneven in timing and effectiveness; customer interactions are tenuous. Contractor is trying hard.
  4. Good - Timely, effective, and courteous. Delivery of services is smooth and organized. Customer problems are resolved.
  5. Excellent - Contractor is timely, has good communications, and acts professional. Provides new ideas, strategy, and analysis to assist the customer.
